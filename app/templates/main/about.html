About Food Waste Tracker

Fighting hunger and reducing waste through technology

Our Mission

To create a sustainable future where no food goes to waste while people go hungry.

Food Waste Tracker is a web-based platform designed to address UN Sustainable Development Goal 2: Zero Hunger. We connect food donors with NGOs and organizations serving vulnerable populations, creating an efficient system to redistribute surplus food.

Our platform enables restaurants, households, grocery stores, and other food donors to easily log their surplus food items, while NGOs can browse, search, and claim these donations for pickup and distribution to those in need.

Making a Difference

Every donation matters in our fight against hunger and food waste.

The Problem

  • 1.3 billion tons of food wasted globally each year
  • Millions suffer from chronic hunger and malnutrition
  • Lack of efficient systems to connect donors with recipients
  • Environmental impact from food waste

Our Solution

  • Centralized platform for food donation management
  • Real-time matching of donors with NGOs
  • User-friendly interface for all stakeholders
  • Efficient tracking and coordination system
