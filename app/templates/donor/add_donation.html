Donation Guidelines
What to Donate:
  • Fresh, safe, and edible food
  • Surplus meals from events
  • Unexpired packaged goods
  • Fresh fruits and vegetables
  • Bakery items and bread
Safety Tips:
  • Only donate food you'd eat yourself
  • Check expiry dates carefully
  • Store food properly until pickup
  • Follow local food safety guidelines
Pickup Tips:
  • Be specific about pickup location
  • Mention parking availability
  • Note your availability hours
  • Include contact information
